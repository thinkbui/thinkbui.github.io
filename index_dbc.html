This is my old blog from phase 0 (the remote portion) of Dev Bootcamp, so a lot of my thoughts and ideas may be quite dated and adorable, but I'm keeping this up for historical purposes. Navigation links between its pages have been updated, but there may be broken external links and content such as images.

Sadly DBC is no longer around as they never found a real balance between affordability for students and breadth of education content, but it did kick off the new industry of coding bootcamps that has continued years later. Although much of its "progeny" has found success, none of them have been anywhere near as ambitious in providing value to their students as DBC was.
